Agonaut is the arena for onchain perps. You trade, you compete, and you get
rewarded for being right.

It is built on [Hyperliquid](https://hyperliquid.xyz), so the liquidity, depth,
and speed are real, not a wrapper. It is self custodial, so your funds never
leave your wallet. And on top of every trade you earn points, climb a weekly
leaderboard, and win USDC.

<Note>
  Agonaut is non custodial. We never hold your funds. Every trade, deposit, and
  withdrawal is signed by your own wallet and settled on Hyperliquid.
</Note>

## How rewards work, in one line

You trade. Agonaut collects a small builder fee on your volume. The large
majority of that fee flows back to the community as competition prizes and
referral payouts. The rest keeps the platform running. See [Fees](/fees) for
the full breakdown.
