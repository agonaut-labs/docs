Agonaut's only fee is a small builder fee on your trading volume. Hyperliquid's
own trading fees apply separately, the same as trading on Hyperliquid directly.

## The builder fee

| | |
| --- | --- |
| Rate | 0.030% of notional volume |
| Charged | At fill time, by Hyperliquid |
| Approved | Once, as a cap, when you activate trading |

You approve the cap up front (see [Activate](/getting-started/activate)). You
are only ever charged on real trades.

## Where the fee goes

The builder fee is how Agonaut earns revenue to run the platform:

  <Card title="Platform" icon="server">

  </Card>
  <Card title="Community" icon="users">
    Separately, Agonaut funds USDC prizes and referral payouts as promotional

<Note>
  There is no token to prop up. Rewards are paid as USDC promotional prizes,
  not an inflationary token.
</Note>

## Other costs

- **Hyperliquid trading fees** apply as normal, separate from the builder fee.
- **Withdrawals** carry a flat 1 USDC fee, taken by Hyperliquid.
- **Deposits** are just transfers, aside from a small one time activation on a
  brand new Hyperliquid account.
