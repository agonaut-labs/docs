Points are how Agonaut measures your week. They power your Points page and,
when competition seasons run, your leaderboard standing, and recognize how
active you are.

<Note>
**Points are not tokens.** They have no monetary value, cannot be transferred
or sold, and do not represent or guarantee any token, airdrop, or future
reward. They simply recognize your activity in the arena. If a distribution
ever happens, it is entirely at our discretion.
</Note>

## How you earn

You earn points by trading. The more volume you trade, the more you earn, and
trading consistently across the week helps too.

## Where to see your points

Your points, rank, volume, and active days are on your Points page. A public
leaderboard appears when a competition season is running.

<Warning>
  Points reflect your trading activity, not a guarantee of any reward.
  Perpetual futures with leverage are high risk: a small adverse move can
  liquidate your position and zero your balance. Competition prizes are
  discretionary promotional rewards, never guaranteed. Trade only at your own
  risk.
</Warning>
