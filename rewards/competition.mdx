The Agonaut competition is coming soon. When it opens, it will run as seasonal
rounds in the arena, where the best-performing traders can win USDC prizes.

## Seasons and weeks

- A **season** is a longer chapter of the competition.
- When seasons are live, each season is made of **weekly rounds**. A new
  leaderboard opens every week, so a strong week can put you near the top even
  if you joined recently.

## The leaderboard

When the competition is live, the leaderboard ranks traders by their trading
performance over the period — trade well to climb. Your activity
[points](/rewards/points) are a separate, always-on system that recognises how
much you trade and feed future rewards.

## Prizes

Prizes are provided by Agonaut as promotional USDC rewards. Prizes for a round
are split across the top of the leaderboard using a power law curve: higher
ranks earn more, but the curve is shaped so that rewards reach a healthy number
of traders, not just the single winner. Everyone who trades is automatically on
the leaderboard; there is no entry fee or buy-in.

<Note>
  Rewards are paid in USDC as promotional prizes from Agonaut. There is no
  Agonaut token. See [Fees](/fees).
</Note>

<Warning>
  Perpetual futures with leverage are high risk — a small adverse move can
  liquidate your position and zero your balance. Competition prizes are
  discretionary promotional rewards, never guaranteed, and never drawn from
  your balance or anyone's trading funds. Trade only at your own risk.
</Warning>
