---
title: "FAQ"
description: "Common questions about custody, fees, rewards, and trading on Agonaut."
---

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Does Agonaut hold my funds?" icon="lock">
    No. Agonaut is non custodial. Your funds stay in your own wallet and settle
    on Hyperliquid. Every action is signed by you. See [Security](/security).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Is there an Agonaut token?" icon="coins">
    No. Rewards are paid in USDC as promotional prizes from Agonaut. There is no
    token, no lockup, and no airdrop points that only pay out in a token later.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What does it cost to trade?" icon="receipt">
    A builder fee of 0.030% on your volume, plus Hyperliquid's own trading fees.
    Agonaut reinvests heavily in the community via USDC promotional rewards. See
    [Fees](/fees).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do I need a crypto wallet?" icon="wallet">
    Not necessarily. You can sign in with email and Agonaut creates a secure
    embedded wallet for you, still self custodial. Or connect a browser wallet
    like MetaMask or Rabby. See [Connect](/getting-started/connect).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I earn rewards?" icon="trophy">
    Trade. Your volume earns points, and everyone who trades earns points
    automatically, with no entry fee. When competition seasons run, the
    best-performing traders can win USDC promotional prizes from Agonaut. You can
    also earn by [referring](/rewards/referrals) other traders.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What is the agent wallet, and is it safe?" icon="bolt">
    It is an optional key that lets orders sign instantly without a popup on
    every action. It can place and cancel orders only. It can never withdraw
    your funds, and you can revoke it any time. See
    [Agent wallet](/trading/agent-wallet).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How long do withdrawals take?" icon="clock">
    Usually around five minutes, settling to a wallet on Arbitrum. A flat 1 USDC
    fee applies. See [Withdraw](/trading/withdraw).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Why can't I see the exact points formula?" icon="calculator">
    The precise weighting is kept private so the system cannot be gamed, the same
    as every serious points program. Trade genuinely and consistently to climb.
    See [Points](/rewards/points).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Which markets can I trade?" icon="chart-candlestick">
    The perpetual markets available on Hyperliquid, including majors and a wider
    set of markets. Each market shows its own chart, order book, and funding.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
